We've got a big night planned to kick off our design show on Friday the 7th:

  • Yellowhead Beer on tap
  • Catering by Culina
  • Music from Sadeeq

The launch event has limited space. So make sure you grab your ticket sooner rather than later.

Bushel is hosting Tête Jaune - its first industrial design show. On Friday night you can meet the designers and makers behind some of Edmonton's best made goods. We are showing and selling everything from one-off prototypes to production-ready goods, from small wares and lighting to furniture.

Tickets are only for Friday November 7th 6pm to 11pm. Saturday and Sunday are free to attend from 9am to 6pm.

Featured designers include: On Our Table | Concrete Cat | Oliver Apt. | IZM | Tomnuk | Chris Brodt | Teng Teng | Nomadic Infrastructure Labs | Bureau for Designations | Loyal Loot | Adam McKertcher | Adam Larson | Britt Reed | Mahsa Karimi | Joshua Smith | David Van Vliet | Arthur Hobden | Nick Kazakoff & Brendan Gallagher | Mike Lam | Mark Andrew Lafond

About Bushel
